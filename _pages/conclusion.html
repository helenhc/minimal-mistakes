  • Myth misinformation users tend to explore around all the subreddits we have chosen, but common users of other subreddits almost never participate in myth subreddits
  • Myth subreddits seem to be incredibly niche
  • Users identified in two myth subreddits tend to have a high political misinformation polarity
  • Political misinformation users appear to be more widespread, since all subreddits have a decently sizeable polarity
  • Political misinformation seems to diffuse more rapidly, while myth is confined within its walls
